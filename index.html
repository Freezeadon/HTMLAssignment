About Me

Biography

Hello! I'm Victor Kwan, an aspiring IT professional currently pursuing an Honours Bachelor of Arts in Information Technology at York University, with an expected graduation date of 2025.

Currently, I serve as a Cyber-Seniors Mentor, where I teach seniors essential computer, smartphone, and internet skills. I focus on online safety, email setup, file organization, and troubleshooting, tailoring my lessons to meet individual learning needs. This volunteer work has allowed me to foster a supportive learning environment and make a meaningful impact helping seniors learn. In addition to my technical skills, I am committed to helping others navigate the digital world safely and confidently. My work with Cyber Seniors demonstrates my passion for teaching and my ability to create personalized, effective learning plans.

I hold several professional certifications, including the OpenEDG Python Institute's Programming with Python Professional Certificate, Zendesk Customer Service Professional Certificate, and Google IT Support Specialization. These certifications reflect my dedication to continuous learning and my proficiency in key technical areas.

Thank you for taking the time to learn more about me. I invite you to connect with me on LinkedIn or reach out via email if you have any questions or opportunities to discuss.
